View My GitHub Profile

I am a software engineer in CDSTIC. I received my Ph.D. from the College of Computer at NUDT supervised by Ting Wang. I was also a Full-Time Visiting Ph.D student in the ILCC at UoE supervised by Miles Osborne. I worked in WSLC in 2014.

My research focuses on applying learning to rank to find Tweets, opinions and people in Twitter. Previously, I worked on keyword extraction in Chinese.

I am also a:

  • AAAI member.
  • Reviewer at EMNLP 2015-2016, CCL 2016, NLPCC 2016, SocialNLP 2016.
  • Student volunteer at ICML 2012 and EMNLP 2011.
