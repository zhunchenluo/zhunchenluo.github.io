I am a software engineer in China Defense Science and Technology Information Center. I received my Ph.D. from the College of Computer at National University of Defense Technology supervised by Ting Wang. I was also a Full-Time Visiting Ph.D student in the ILCC at University of Edinburgh supervised by Miles Osborne. I worked in Wenchang Satellite Launch Center in 2014.

My research focuses on applying learning to rank to find Tweets, opinions and people in Twitter. Previously, I worked on keyword extraction in Chinese.

I am also a:

  • Member of Chinese Information Processing Society of China, Youth Working Committee.
  • Member of AAAI.
  • PC Member at EACL 2017, EMNLP 2015-2016, SocialNLP 2016-2017, NLPCC 2016, CCL 2016.
  • Student volunteer at ICML 2012 and EMNLP 2011.
